About

Welcome to MindScape – Psychology Meets Curiosity

Here, you can explore personality traits, behavior patterns, emotional intelligence, stress responses, and cognitive styles through interactive tests. This is a casual curiosity project—I do not have a formal psychology background, and my knowledge comes from reading a few books and personal interest.

MindScape is designed for self-reflection, learning, and exploration, not for clinical or medical purposes. Discover more about yourself, your habits, and how you interact with the world, all in a simple and interactive way.
